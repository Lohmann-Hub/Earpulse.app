Features

Everything you need
to track your heart.

A native iOS app that puts your health first — without cluttering your Apple Health app.

📈

Real-Time Heart Rate

Live heart rate display with animated EKG waveform. Instant BPM updates with manual start/stop controls for precise monitoring sessions.

📊

History & Analytics

7-day overview with Apple-style charts. Session statistics showing Average, Minimum, and Maximum BPM — filterable by activity or data source.

🏷

Activity Tagging

Tag your heart rate sessions across 7 predefined activity types. Understand your heart across every moment of your day.

🍎

Apple Fitness Integration

Seamlessly combines Earpulse and Apple Watch workout data in a single unified view. Full HealthKit integration — data stored securely.

✦

Beautiful Design

Apple Health-inspired interface with Light, Dark, and Tinted icon modes. Smooth animations and responsive UI across all iPhone sizes.

☁️

iCloud Data Safety

All data stays in your personal iCloud. No third-party cloud sync. No unnecessary data collection. Your health data, your control.
